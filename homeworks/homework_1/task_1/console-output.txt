Hello, Scala! Sashka here~                                                                                           
scala> var Spanishmode = helloMessage.replaceFirst("Hello", "Hola")

Hola, Scala! Sashka here~                                                                                            
scala> var german = helloMessage.replaceFirst("Hello", "Guten Tag")

scala> var name = "Sashka"

Hello, Scala! akhsaS here
